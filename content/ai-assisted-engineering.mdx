# The Shift to AI-Powered Delivery

In the highly regulated space of Digital Banking, security and compliance often dictate delivery velocity. However, by carefully integrating AI tools into our engineering workflows, we've managed to significantly boost productivity without compromising our rigorous standards.

## Secure Adoption of AI

We evaluate tools like:
- **GitHub Copilot** for real-time autocomplete
- **Claude Code** for complex architecture refactoring
- **Local LLMs** for processing sensitive data internally

## Measuring Impact

Since adopting these tools, our PR review times have decreased by 30%, and boilerplate generation has become near-instant. The key to this success is providing engineers with clear guardrails and automated security checks.
